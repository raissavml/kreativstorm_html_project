Rock Climbing

This sport was very important to me during my teenage years and I never stopped loving it!

I was able to experience 2 types of climbing, bouldering and Sport climbing. Here are the definition of each:

  • Bouldering:

    Bouldering is a branch of rock climbing where people climb without ropes or harnesses over safety mats at low heights.

    Click here for more information about bouldering.

  • Sport climbing

    Is the act of climbing single- or multi-pitch routes, protected by permanently-fixed bolts and anchors drilled into the rock, using a rope and the aid of a belayer.
