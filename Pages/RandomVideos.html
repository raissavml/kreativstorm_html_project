Random Videos

Do you like to learn weird things? The kind you will never use? Please, join me!

  • Metal restoration

    Watch the process of metal objects restoration is really satisfying! The rust goes away with electrolysis and sandblasting, painting all the parts, polishing.. Seeing the object regain its form.

  • Hoof trimming

    Have you ever watched a hoof trimming? There is so much things involving the health of a cows hoof, the type of floor, the pressure on each digit, the season...

  • Object design assessment

    Have you ever thought about all the details a designer need to think about when mcreating a product? The handle has a shape easy and comfortable to hold? Someone more weak, or with slippery hands will be able to use it? The marks and letters are easy to see? This assessments make you choose your new products with another eyes.
